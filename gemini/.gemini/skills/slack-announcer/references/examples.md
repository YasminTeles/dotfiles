# Slack Announcement Examples

## Example 1: New Feature Announcement (v5.1.0)

Hello everyone!

We are excited to announce a new feature in our Diagnostics library v5.1.0: Events.

What are Events?

Events are structured, severity-less logs designed to capture meaningful business or system occurrences. Think of them as a way to record "what happened" rather than just logging errors or debug information. This allows for better tracking of key actions, like user logins, purchases, or feature usage, without cluttering your standard logs.

This new feature will help us gain better insights into how our applications are being used.
